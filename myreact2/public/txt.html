This Project

FM Vault is a user-friendly web application crafted by the talented team at Caraxes. This platform offers a seamless FM radio experience, allowing users to choose from popular stations such as Hello FM, Big FM, Radio Mirchi, and Thendral FM. With a simple drop-down menu, users can effortlessly select their preferred station and enjoy uninterrupted streaming. FM Vault not only provides easy access to diverse radio content but also ensures a smooth and enjoyable listening experience for users of all backgrounds. It's a testament to Caraxes' commitment to creating accessible and engaging software solutions.
